# PRIVACY POLICY

**Last update:** 06/06/2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the **NOYA** app, developed and made available by **Fabiano de Almeida e Oliveira**, manages and protects your information. Our commitment is to ensure maximum transparency and respect for your privacy.

## 1. Data Collection and Use

**NOYA** is a tool dedicated to personal finance management. To protect your privacy, we **do not collect** personally identifiable information such as your name, e-mail address, phone number, or identification documents. No account creation is required to use our services.

The only information processed by the App is the income and expense data you voluntarily enter. This data is used solely and exclusively for the core functionality of the App, such as generating balances, reports, and charts on your screen.

## 2. Local Data Storage

All financial information recorded by you is stored **exclusively locally** on your own mobile device. We do not have access to your financial data, and we do not send it to our own servers or external databases automatically.

## 3. Backup Functionality

To prevent data loss in case of formatting, theft, or device replacement, the App provides a backup feature.

Exporting or sending data outside your device (for example to the user’s cloud storage or file storage) only occurs **through a direct, explicit, and manual action by the user**. No data is transferred in the background without your authorization.

## 4. Data Sharing with Third Parties

Since your financial records remain on your device, we **do not share, sell, or transfer** any information entered in the App to third parties, analytics companies, or advertising networks.

## 5. Requested Permissions

To function correctly and allow manual backup execution, the App may require access to specific operating system permissions, such as:

* **Storage:** To save the financial database on your device and create the backup file.
* **Internet Access:** Used exclusively to enable file transfer when the user triggers the backup function.

## 6. Security

The security of your information depends on the security of your own device. We recommend that you use strong screen lock passwords and keep your operating system up to date to protect physical access to the app.

## 7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically to reflect improvements to the app. Any changes will take effect immediately after the new policy is published on this same page, with the "Last update" date revised.

## 8. Contact

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy or the operation of the app, contact the developer **Fabiano de Almeida e Oliveira** via e-mail: **contato@fabiano.dev**.
